Introducing Organizational DNA (ODNA), the structure perspective has changed from the person to the Organization. We have developed a product that works with active directory to automate permissions and changes as they occur in organizations. it allows users to get the data and access they need without the data and access they don't. It is user friendly and requires no on-going IT intervention. What used to take weeks, takes hours, or minutes. What used to be a very painful manual process is completely automated, and includes drag and drop capabilities for moving people, and for making large company changes such as mergers, acquisitions, and reorganizations. What used to be a very complicated, and many times incorrect and overwhelming set of groups in AD is now, in this product a clean set of groups that are automatically changed as changes are made to the organization – on either a large or small scale.

In this illustration each umbrella is representative of a user ID in Azure Active Directory or Active Directly. Every color group is representative of a specific company or division of an organizational structure, every sub-color group represents a department. Where each person is standing is representative of their location, and each small group of umbrella’s represents an area. In AAD/AD today each group must be manually setup or completed with scripts. Each different person (user ID), color (division), sub-color group (department), standing position (location), and small groups of umbrella’s (areas) would have to be created manually. As the organization changes, these groups need to be reorganized, and the people manually affected. 2 million and counting. EVERY ONE must be removed, and the groups corrected. It is rarely correct or clean, even using scripts. Billions and Billions are dollars are spent, and the result is far less than reliable.

We have developed a product that works with active directory to automate permissions and changes as they occur in organizations. it allows users to get the data and access they need without the data and access they don't. It is user friendly, and requires no on-going IT intervention.

ODNA, LLC

Each umbrella is representative of a userID in Active Directory (AD). Every color group is representative of a specific division, every sub-color group represents a department of an organizational structure. Where each person is standing is representative of their location, and each small group of umbrella’s represents an area. In AD today each group must be manually setup. Each different person (userID), color (division), sub-color group (department), standing position (location), and small groups of umbrella’s (areas) would have to be created manually. As the organization changes, these groups need to be reorganized, and in essence the people manually moved to the appropriate group(s). This process generally leaves organizations with a great deal of work, and full time IT staff to control the groups and make the changes. They can do this effectively if they get the information from management or operations, and in a timely manner.
